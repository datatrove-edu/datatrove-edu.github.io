This is the color legend for the maps:

| Color    | Land Cover | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Pink     | Low-intensity urban | Less densely developed areas (some vegetation present) |
| Red      | Impervious surface | Developed/built-up land — roads, rooftops, pavement |
| Dark red | High-intensity urban | Most densely developed areas |
| Tan      | Shrub/scrub | Dominant surrounding desert; shrubs under 5 m tall |
